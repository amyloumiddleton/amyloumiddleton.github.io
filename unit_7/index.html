E T H E R E A L - L A N D S C A P E S

This debut collection of art & craft has been carefully curated to explore the theme of ethereal landscapes. When we think of the term ethereal we often think of that which is delicate and light, the unexplained, something that seems not to be of this world. It is often left in the hands of artists and craftsman alike to make that which appears intangible into something that is tangible giving us access to a new found territory.
